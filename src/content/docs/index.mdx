## Why use our design system?

- **Productivity**: Build UIs faster with pre-built, tested components
- **Consistency**: Maintain visual harmony across your applications
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 compliant components out of the box
- **Flexibility**: Easily customize and extend to match your needs
- **Dark Mode**: Built-in dark theme support for modern applications
